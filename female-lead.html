Female Lead

Prompt: If you were to hire a strong female lead, who would it be? What personality or physical characteristics motivate your choice?

If I were to hire a strong female lead for a Hollywood movie, it would be Taraji P. Henson. I'd hire Taraji because I was very impressed with her work as a strong female lead in Hidden Figures. Personality wise, I would want my female lead to be smart, assertive, and empowered, while also having the emotional intelligence to be conscientious and kind when interacting with others.

When I think of a strong character, I think more in terms of the personality characteristics than the physical. I don't have any specific body types or other physical traits in mind. But because this is a Hollywood movie, I suppose the actress would need to be someone that is generally accepted as attractive by the public, which I'd say that Taraji P. Henson is.
